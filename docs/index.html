Hi, I'm Kseniya!

I am a student in College LaSalle studying video game programming. I develop games in Unity (C#) and Unreal Engine (Blueprint and C++). I come from Ukraine and I've lived in Montreal since 2009. My passion for game development comes from my inner curiosity of always wanting to know how games work behind the curtains. My main areas of focus are the gameplay mechanics and UI programming. I also like to help doing level design and animation, if possible, which are my other interests besides programming. Check out my school projects and game jams below!
